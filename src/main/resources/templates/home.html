Flashreport CloudFoundry Spring sample

This sample application lets you generate reports using the flashreport api.
Unless you inject the VCAP_SERVICES environment variable yourself, it will only work correctly when deployed on CloudFoundry and bound to a flashreport service.

Generate a report

Use the /report/new endpoint to generate a report. For your convenience, this endpoint support both GET and POST.
From there you can use two other endpoints : /report/uuid and /report/uuid/download, where uuid is the unique identifier of the report you generated.

Keep in mind that the number of reports you can generate is limited based on the plan you selected in the marketplace. Attempting to generate reports when over the limit will result in 429 responses.
